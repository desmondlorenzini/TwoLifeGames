Privacy Policy

Two Life Games does not collect any data from its users, with some exceptions below:

If you play Speed-Kill and upload a level in the level creator, it will be uploaded to Unity's UGC service and made publicly available to other players in-game. This level data will be stored and saved to the cloud.

By uploading a level, you accept that we may remove, alter, or moderate your content for any purpose we see fit.

If you play Probending Arena, we use Meta's InAppPurchases API to track who buys the in app purchases in our game. If you want your data to be deleted, contact us at: desmondlorenzini@gmail.com
